Welcome — this policy explains how Love Bite Restaurant ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you interact with our website or services. We follow applicable privacy laws including the GDPR and CCPA and are committed to being transparent about our practices.

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to process orders and reservations, deliver services, respond to inquiries, send transactional messages, and (with your permission) send promotional emails. We also use data to improve our website, perform analytics, and maintain the security of our systems. We do not use personal data for unrelated purposes without your consent.

Retention: We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Retention periods vary by data type; contact us to request details.

Sharing & Third Parties

We may share information with service providers necessary to run our business (payment processors, delivery partners, analytics providers). All third parties are required to handle data securely and only for the purposes we specify. We do not sell personal information to third-party advertisers.

Data Security

We protect data using industry-standard measures such as TLS (HTTPS) for data in transit and reasonable controls for data at rest. Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel and vendors. We regularly assess our security controls and perform audits to reduce risk.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are an EU or California resident you have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data and to object to certain processing. To make a request, email lovebiterestaurant89@gmail.com with "Data Request" in the subject and include identifying details. We will verify requests to protect privacy and respond within applicable timeframes.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for essential site functionality, performance analytics (for example, Google Analytics), and optional personalization. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser; to learn more about cookies we use and how to opt out, contact lovebiterestaurant89@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will promptly delete it.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new effective date and, where required, notified to you by email.
