Love Bite Restaurant - Security Terms

Security Terms

Last updated: April 14 2026

Love Bite Restaurant is committed to protecting your data and ensuring a secure experience. This page explains the measures we take, your responsibilities, and how to report concerns related to the security of our services.

Security Measures

We use industry-standard controls to protect your information:

  • Encryption: TLS/HTTPS encrypts data in transit. Where feasible, we apply encryption at rest (e.g., AES-256).
  • Access Controls: Administrative access is limited, logged, and protected by multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  • Monitoring & Audits: We run regular vulnerability scans and security reviews to identify and remediate risks.
  • Backups & Recovery: Encrypted backups are stored securely and restore procedures are tested periodically.
  • Payments: Payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS-compliant third parties (e.g., Stripe).

User Responsibilities

Help us keep your account secure by using strong passwords, enabling 2FA where available, and reporting suspicious activity. Avoid sharing account credentials and keep devices up to date with security patches.

Incident Response & Notifications

We maintain an incident response plan. If an incident occurs, we will investigate, contain the issue, and notify affected users as required by law, typically within 72 hours when applicable.

Third-Party Vendors

We evaluate and monitor third-party vendors for appropriate security controls. However, we cannot guarantee the practices of external providers — review their policies when using integrated services.

Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you discover a vulnerability, please report it responsibly to lovebiterestaurant89@gmail.com. We appreciate responsible disclosures and will respond promptly.
